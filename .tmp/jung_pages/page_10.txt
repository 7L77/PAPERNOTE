REPRODUCIBILITY
In order to ensure reproducibility, we build our dataset on architectures from a common NAS-
Benchmark, which is described in the main paper in subsection 3.1. In addition, all hyperparameters
for reproducing the robustness results are given in subsection 3.2. Lastly, a complete description of
the dataset itself is provided in the appendix, Appendix A.
